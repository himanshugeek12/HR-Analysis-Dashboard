# HR-Analysis-Dashboard (Interactive Dashboard creation using MS Excel)

## Project Objective
The objective of the HR Analysis Dashboard is to analyze employee distribution, performance, salary insights, and workforce trends across departments and regions. This dashboard helps HR teams understand employee demographics, optimize workforce planning, and improve decision-making.

## Dataset used 
- <a href="https://github.com/himanshugeek12/HR-Analysis-Dashboard/blob/master/HR%20Analysts-Excel%20Dashboard.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## ❓ Key Questions (KPIs)
- What is the **total number of employees**?
- What is the **gender distribution (Male vs Female)**?
- How are **employees distributed across departments**?
- What is the **leave taken by different job roles**?
- What is the **salary distribution by job title**?
- How are **employees distributed across regions**?
- What is the **employment status breakdown (Full-time, Part-time, Contract)**?
- What are the **age group distributions of employees**?
- What is the **work location distribution (Office, Remote, Branch)**?
- How do **skills vary across employees**?

- Dashboard Interaction <a href="https://github.com/himanshugeek12/HR-Analysis-Dashboard/blob/master/HR-Analysis-Dashboard.png">View Dashboard</a>

## 🔄 Process
- Verified dataset for missing values and inconsistencies.
- Cleaned and transformed raw HR data into structured format.
- Created calculated fields for:
  - Total Employees  
  - Salary Distribution  
  - Leave Tracking  
  - Employee Demographics  
- Built interactive visuals for:
  - Gender distribution  
  - Department-wise filtering  
  - Salary by job roles  
  - Region-wise employee count  
  - Employment status breakdown  
  - Age group analysis  
- Applied slicers for dynamic filtering (Gender, Department).

## 🔍 Project Insights
- **Total employees are 50**, with a slightly higher **female workforce (52%)** compared to males (48%).
- **HR Specialists and Managers** show higher salary contributions.
- **Designers and Managers** have the highest leave counts.
- **Central region** has the highest number of employees.
- Majority of employees are **full-time (20)** compared to part-time and contract roles.
- **Branch office (46%)** is the most common work location.
- Employees are mostly concentrated in the **26–45 age group**.
- **Communication and Design** are the top skills among employees.

## ✅ Final Conclusion  
The **HR Analysis Dashboard** provides comprehensive insights into employee distribution, performance, and organizational structure. It enables HR teams to make data-driven decisions by identifying workforce trends, optimizing resource allocation, and improving overall organizational efficiency.
